How to choose ice cream

Create your ice cream your own way

Choose the style of ice cream. There are different styles of ice cream, so you can choose the one you like. *Prices vary depending on the type of style.

Once you have chosen everything, come to our store and you are all set! *You can't order ice cream on the website, so you must order it from the store.

What are you interested in?

Eating Contest

We start the Ice Cream Eating Contest every year on April 6th. This competition has been running steadily for a long time and every year a new champion is awarded with prizes and vouchers that can be used in our store. The rule is that the person who eats the ice cream as fast as possible in 5 minutes wins. Anyone can participate and if you are interested, please contact us by email.

Yard Sale

There will be a yard sale on June 14, 2022. We have prepared an event for customers to have a good time by talking to each other through yard sales. If you are interested in selling individual items, you must notify us by e-mail in advance. The time starts at 2 P.M in our parking lot. Thank you for your interest.

Birthday

We treat our guests' anniversaries as our own. Started in 2021, we offer free ice cream on your birthday. You can only get one of any size, and you just need to bring a license to prove your birthday. You must come on the day of your birthday!

Free Ice Cream

At the beginning of each year in March, we are holding an event where we are offering free ice cream to each person. This event has been started since we first opened and is also a day to commemorate the opening day of our store. Remember the date and get free ice cream every March! The way to get free ice cream is to visit our store and say hello!

We are an ice cream shop that keeps a long tradition and always does its best. We have various flavors of ice cream at reasonable prices, and the best part is that it always guarantees fresh taste. This website covers our content, menus, addresses and events. If you would like to inquire personally, please send a text message to us by e-mail. We check our emails Monday through Friday, so you may receive a reply from us a day or two after we receive your email. To keep our guests having fun and sharing the joy, we offer events and update them from time to time. Waiting for the day to meet everyone, we will continue to keep this place.
